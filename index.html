Our Eternal Companions

These four souls transcend ownership—they’re a shared treasure. Leo guards us from beyond, while Melo, Tommy, and Jerry weave their magic in our lives.

Leo – The Celestial Guardian

Departed September 13, 2022, Leo’s heroic sacrifice against dark forces echoes in eternity.

Leo was our protector, a celestial warrior who faced black magic to save us. The jinns took her life, but her spirit remains our shield. She’d perch on high, eyes glowing with resolve, chasing away shadows with her fierce love.

Melo – The Midnight Muse

Melo’s girlish charm and soft voice defy his boyish form, a delicate enigma.

Melo moves with a feminine grace—his high-pitched meows and dainty steps surprise everyone. He loves stealing socks from new visitors, hiding them under the couch so they’ll stay longer. His black-and-white coat is his cloak of mystery.

Tommy – The Amber Storyteller

Tommy’s loud demands and entertaining antics light up the realm with warmth.

Tommy’s a showman—when he wants food or attention, his booming “DUH” echoes through the house. Yet his cuddly side shines through, curling up with a purr that vibrates the room. He’s been known to swipe keys and hide them to keep guests captive!

Jerry – The Wild Wanderer

Jerry’s silent presence erupts into shouts for food or affection, a loyal trickster.

Jerry’s the quiet one—until he’s hungry or missing someone, then his yowls pierce the silence. He’ll latch onto new people, stealing their stuff (like wallets!) and stashing it away so they can’t leave. His brownish hues flicker with wild energy.
